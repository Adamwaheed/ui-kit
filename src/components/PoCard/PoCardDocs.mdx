### With title and action

You should always use Card action with the title. Otherwise it would not be displayed properly.

    <div className="po-p-5 po-bg-slate-100">
    	<Story of={PoCardStories.WithTitleAndAction} />
    </div>

<Source
	language="js"
	dark
	format={true}
	code={dedent`
    <PoCard title="Card title" class="p-5">
        <template v-slot:action>
          <PoButton label="Create" />
        </template>
        <template v-slot:content>
        Content
        </template>
    </PoCard>
  `}
/>
